# Shipping Policy

**Effective Date:** 17 June 2026
**Last Updated:** 17 June 2026

This Shipping Policy applies to Smart Door nameplate hardware orders
placed on mysmartdoor.in.

---

## 1. Shipping Coverage

We currently ship across India. International shipping is not available at this time.

## 2. Processing Time

- Orders are manufactured to order (engraved with your details and Plate ID)
- Manufacturing typically takes **2–4 business days** after payment confirmation
- You will receive a notification when your order enters manufacturing and again when it ships

## 3. Delivery Timelines

| Region | Estimated Delivery |
|---|---|
| Bhopal / Madhya Pradesh | 2–4 business days after dispatch |
| Major metro cities | 4–6 business days after dispatch |
| Rest of India | 5–8 business days after dispatch |

Timelines are estimates and may vary due to courier delays, weather, or regional disruptions.

## 4. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges (if any) are displayed at checkout before payment. We may offer free shipping promotions from time to time.

## 5. Order Tracking

Once dispatched, you will receive a tracking link via email/WhatsApp. You can also check order status from your Smart Door dashboard.

## 6. Delivery Issues

- **Failed delivery attempts:** The courier will attempt redelivery; please ensure your address and phone number are accurate
- **Lost in transit:** Contact support — we will investigate with the courier and arrange a replacement or refund
- **Wrong address provided by customer:** Re-shipping charges may apply

## 7. Damaged Packages

If your package arrives visibly damaged, please refuse delivery or note it with the courier, and contact **support@mysmartdoor.in** within 48 hours with photos.
