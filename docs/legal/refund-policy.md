## 1. Hardware Orders (Smart Nameplates)

- **Cancellation before dispatch:** Full refund if you cancel before the order is dispatched for manufacturing/shipping
- **Cancellation after dispatch:** Not eligible for refund, but may be eligible for return (see below)
- **Defective or damaged on arrival:** Full refund or free replacement if reported within **7 days** of delivery, with photo/video evidence
- **Wrong item shipped:** Full refund or replacement at no extra cost

## 2. Returns

- Returns are accepted within **7 days** of delivery for defective or damaged products only
- The nameplate must be returned in its original packaging, undamaged by the customer
- Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are processed within **7–10 business days** to the original payment method

## 3. Subscription Refunds

- Subscription fees are **non-refundable** once the billing cycle has started, except where required by law
- If you cancel a subscription, you retain access until the end of the current paid period — no partial refunds for unused time
- If a renewal payment is captured in error (e.g., duplicate charge), contact support for a full refund within 5 business days

## 4. Non-Refundable Situations

- Change of mind after dispatch (hardware orders)
- Damage caused by misuse, improper installation, or unauthorized modification
- Subscription time already used

## 5. How to Request a Refund

Email **support@smartdoor.in** with your order ID, reason for refund, and (if applicable) photo/video evidence. We aim to respond within **48 hours**.

## 6. Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed via Razorpay to your original payment method within **7–10 business days**, depending on your bank.
