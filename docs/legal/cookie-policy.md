## 1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a
website. They help the site remember information about your visit.

## 2. How We Use Cookies

| Type | Purpose | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| **Strictly Necessary** | Required for login sessions, security, and core functionality | Session tokens, CSRF protection |
| **Functional** | Remember preferences | Language, "remember this device" for trusted login |
| **Analytics** | Understand how visitors use our site | Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity, Plausible |
| **Marketing** (if enabled) | Measure campaign performance | Ad platform pixels, if used |

We do not use cookies to sell your data to third parties.

## 3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services we use:
- **Google Analytics** — usage analytics
- **Microsoft Clarity** — session heatmaps/recordings (no payment or PIN data captured)
- **Razorpay** — payment processing security
- **Plausible** — privacy-friendly analytics (cookieless by design, where enabled)

## 4. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling
strictly necessary cookies may prevent you from logging in or using core
features of the Service.

## 5. Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" signal. We currently do not respond
differently to DNT signals, but you can manage tracking via your browser
and the opt-outs listed above.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Material changes will
