# Acceptable Use Policy

**Effective Date:** 17 June 2026
**Last Updated:** 17 June 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") sets out rules for using the Smart
Door platform ("Service"). It supplements our [Terms of Service](/legal/terms-of-service.html).

---

## 1. Prohibited Activities

You agree not to:
- Use the Service for any unlawful purpose, harassment, stalking, or surveillance of individuals without consent
- Use call masking or number-routing features to deceive, scam, or defraud visitors or callers
- Attempt to access another user's account, plate, or admin systems without authorization
- Reverse-engineer, scrape, or attempt to bypass rate limits, RLS policies, or PIN lockout protections
- Upload abusive, threatening, defamatory, or illegal content via voice notes, visitor messages, or support tickets
- Use the Service to send unsolicited bulk communications (spam)
- Interfere with or disrupt the integrity or performance of the Service, including attempting denial-of-service attacks
- Impersonate Smart Door staff or another user

## 2. Visitor Data Responsibility

As a nameplate owner, you are responsible for ensuring your use of
visitor logging (photos, voice notes) complies with applicable privacy
laws in your jurisdiction, including obtaining any consent required for
recording visitors at your property.

## 3. Fair Use

Voice note storage, AI message summarization, and call routing are
subject to fair-use limits described in your subscription plan.
Excessive or automated use beyond normal household usage patterns may be
rate-limited or flagged for review.

## 4. Enforcement

Violation of this AUP may result in:
- Warning and required remediation
- Temporary suspension of account or specific features
- Permanent account termination for serious or repeated violations
- Reporting to law enforcement where required by law

## 5. Reporting Abuse

If you believe another user is misusing the Service, contact
**support@mysmartdoor.in** with details.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this AUP from time to time. Continued use of the Service
after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
